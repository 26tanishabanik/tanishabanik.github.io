Hello

I have always been seeking for building new things using the available skillset I have, right from my childhood. I use to play with my big box of a computer all by myself. I also had a habit of opening and dismantling every hardware device I saw, like the radio. To me, programming has always been like playing games. Only you have to play it with improvement over several chances. Programming in itself gives a sense of satisfaction to think about a project and implement it in real-life. I feel myself no less than a consulting detective while searching for the bugs and predicting their possible reasons. In some way my laziness also ingrained me with the will to automate everything, hence programming came to my rescue. I like observing things and the related problems and that is why I try fixing basic problems that I notice using the available skillset I have and that is the thought behind most of my projects.
My primary skills include developing DataScience , Computer Vision and Deep Learning applications.
